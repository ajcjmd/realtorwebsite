BUYING & SELLING IN TODAY'S MARKET


If you're selling your home, chances are you'll be looking to buy as well. That can feel unsettling with today's high-priced, high-interest-rate housing marketing. 1) You're trying to figure out if the high value of your home is worth getting a new loan with a new rate (and chances are it's higher); 2) If you find it's worth doing, do you sell first? If so, where do you go if you can't buy right away?

These are just a few of the questions you're trying to navigate in today's market but don't worry - that's what I'm here for! Give me a call and let's review all the pros and cons, the current trends, predictions for 2023, and everything else you need to know in order to make an informed decision.
